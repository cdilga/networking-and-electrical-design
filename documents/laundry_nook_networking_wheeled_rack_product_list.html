Sourcing and import notes

A few procurement points that matter before you spend money.

AliExpress

Current indexed AliExpress hits were weak for this exact brief. There are plenty of generic castor listings, but the finished cabinet and trolley options were not especially coherent or confidence-inspiring compared with local AU stock and OEM channels.

OEM route

If you do want to import, I would lean toward OEM factory or wholesale channels for either a custom welded frame or just a better wheel package, rather than gambling on a random consumer listing that cannot show real load-path details.

Made-in-China mobile rack example Alibaba retractable caster example

Used-market wildcard

Medical case carts and instrument trolleys often hit the right footprint and have genuinely nice large castors, but they are usually sold by quote or through surplus channels. If you want the smoothest premium wheel feel, used hospital carts are worth watching even if they are not the cheapest path.
