Tower-cart envelope

Design the wheeled rack as a self-supporting unit, not hanging from the network cabinet. Keep it to 980W x 600D until final site verification. Use a vertical envelope based on the chosen wall box: 2015H under 6U, 1880H under 9U, or 1750H under 12U when the cabinet top is at 2440mm. A two-tower cart can fit as either side-by-side tower bays or a two-level stack, provided each bay is kept inside a nominal 270W x 600D x 620H allowance and top/rear cable clearances are maintained.

Site and builder checks before committing

Confirm the actual finished 1040mm wall segment after plasterboard, skirting, door jamb/architrave and any laundry joinery changes. For a 9U/12U wall box, ask for blocking/noggins or a plywood backboard before sheeting; do not rely on plasterboard anchors. If the cabinet is loaded with high-wattage 10GbE equipment, plan top/bottom cabinet fans and keep the cabinet top at least 150-200mm below the ceiling. Ceiling extraction must avoid trusses/insulation and should be agreed before lining.
